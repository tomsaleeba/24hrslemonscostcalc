Cost calculator for 24hrs of LeMons Australia

A tool to help you calculate how many drivers you need so everyone gets enough seat time but doesn't have a cry about not having enough money. Prices taken from http://24lemons.com.au/cough-up-or-shut-up-2/. Yeah, it's not rocket science working out cost but this makes it easier.

I'm not affiliated with 24hrs of LeMons, I was just thinking of entering and thought this would answer a question that a lot of people will ask. View the code for this at https://github.com/tomsaleeba/24hrslemonscostcalc.
